About me

Paul Ian is a Firenze based photographer who has been doing paid gigs for over a year now. His passion for photography began in the year 2004 using his Dad's old Olympus film camera. After some years and the rising popularity of DSLRs, he eventually went digital with Canon. Versatility in the style of shooting is his forte'. He shoots with natural light as much as possible and when circumstances agree, but whenever called for, can also shoot with studio lighting setups even outdoors using off camera speedlites. He prefers a photojournalistic approach when documenting events to give pictures a more natural feel but can also do staged/posed shoots for a stylized look.
